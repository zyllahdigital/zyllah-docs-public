Prompt de
Pesquisa de Prospect

Cole este prompt no Gemini com o nome do prospect. O Gemini faz toda a pesquisa. Você traz o resultado para o Claude fazer o diagnóstico estratégico.

Prompt principal
Copiar e substituir o campo em dourado
You are a research assistant helping me analyze the digital presence of healthcare professionals in Nova Friburgo, RJ, Brazil. I need a structured report to evaluate if they are a good prospect for a premium digital presence agency. Research this professional thoroughly: Name: [NOME COMPLETO DO PROSPECT] Specialty: [ESPECIALIDADE] City: Nova Friburgo, RJ, Brazil Please research and report on each of the following, being specific and factual. If you cannot find information, say "Not found" — do NOT invent or assume: 1. INSTAGRAM - Handle (username) - Number of followers - Number of posts - Approximate date of most recent post - Visual consistency of feed (generic/cohesive/professional) - Bio quality (vague/clear/specialized) - Link in bio (yes/no, what it links to) 2. GOOGLE PRESENCE - Does a Google Business Profile exist? (yes/no) - Star rating and number of reviews - Does the professional appear on page 1 when searching "[specialty] Nova Friburgo"? - Any other notable Google results (Doctoralia, CFM, site, etc.) 3. LINKEDIN - Profile exists? (yes/no) - Headline quality (generic like "Médico" or specific and differentiated) - Number of connections - Recent activity (yes/no) 4. WEBSITE - Own website exists? (yes/no) - URL if found - Quality assessment (generic/professional/premium) - Mobile-friendly? (yes/no/unknown) 5. NOTABLE CREDENTIALS OR DIFFERENTIATORS - Any awards, recognitions, or notable credentials found - Institutional affiliations (hospitals, clinics, universities) - Any media appearances or publications found 6. DIGITAL PRESENCE GAPS - List the 3 most significant gaps between this professional's real-world reputation and their online presence Format your response as a structured report with clear sections. Be concise and factual. Do not add opinions or recommendations — just the facts. I will analyze the strategic implications separately.
Usar no Gemini 2.0 Flash ou superior em gemini.google.com. O Gemini vai pesquisar a web automaticamente. Se ele não encontrar o Instagram, peça explicitamente: "Search specifically for the Instagram account of [nome] dermatologist Nova Friburgo Brazil."
Fluxo de uso
Como usar na prática
1
Abrir gemini.google.com → novo chat.
2
Copiar o prompt acima → substituir [NOME COMPLETO] e [ESPECIALIDADE] pelo prospect.
3
Aguardar o relatório. Cada pesquisa leva 30–60 segundos.
4
Copiar o relatório gerado pelo Gemini → trazer para o Claude com a mensagem: "Aqui está o relatório do Gemini sobre [nome]. Faça o diagnóstico estratégico."
5
Claude analisa o relatório → indica risco de abordagem → ajusta a mensagem de contato com base nos fatos reais encontrados.
Variação rápida — pesquisa em lote
Para pesquisar vários prospects de uma vez
You are a research assistant. I need you to research the digital presence of multiple healthcare professionals in Nova Friburgo, RJ, Brazil. For each professional, provide a brief structured report covering: Instagram (handle, followers, last post date), Google Business Profile (yes/no, rating, reviews), own website (yes/no, URL), LinkedIn (yes/no, active), and the 2 most significant digital presence gaps. Be factual and concise. Say "Not found" when you cannot find something. Do not invent data. Professionals to research: 1. [Nome] — [Especialidade] 2. [Nome] — [Especialidade] 3. [Nome] — [Especialidade] Format as a table or structured list, one section per professional.
Use esta variação para pesquisar 3–5 prospects de uma vez quando quiser agilizar. O relatório fica menos detalhado, mas serve para triagem rápida de quem vale investigar mais.
